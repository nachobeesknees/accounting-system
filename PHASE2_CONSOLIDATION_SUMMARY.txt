TECHNICAL APPROACH
================================================================================

1. INTERCOMPANY MATCHING
   
   Algorithm:
   - Pair discovery: Find entries likely representing same transaction
     * By reference number
     * By date + amount correlation
     * By explicit pair_id links
   - Validation: Check amount, currency, date for each pair
   - Tolerance: Configurable per entity pair (default 0% = exact match)
   - Result: Status (matched | mismatched | unmatched) with detail
   
   Example:
   OpCo1: Dr AR (IC) $1,000, Cr Revenue (IC) $1,000
   OpCo2: Dr Expense (IC) $1,000, Cr AP (IC) $1,000
   → Status: MATCHED (exact match in amount, currency, date)

2. CONSOLIDATION ROLL-UP

   Algorithm:
   - For each entity in scope:
     * Get trial balance (sum of all posted journal lines) as of date
     * For each account, look up consolidation mapping (entity → group)
     * Translate balance to reporting currency using account type:
       - Balance sheet accounts (assets/liabilities): current rate (period-end)
       - Income statement (revenue/expense): average rate for period
       - Equity: historical rate (date of contribution)
     * Aggregate into consolidated trial balance
   
   Result: {consolidation_account_id: balance} for all accounts

3. CURRENCY TRANSLATION (ASC 830)

   Rates:
   - Spot rate: Transaction date (posting)
   - Current rate: Period-end (balance sheet translation)
   - Average rate: Weighted daily average (income statement translation)
   - Historical rate: Date of equity contribution (equity translation)
   
   Result:
   - Assets/Liabilities translated at current rate
   - Revenue/Expense translated at average rate
   - Equity translated at historical rate
   - CTA (plug) flows to OCI

4. ELIMINATION ADJUSTMENTS

   Types:
   - Intercompany AR/AP: Zero out matching receivables/payables
   - Intercompany revenue/expense: Eliminate intra-group activity
   - Investment in subsidiary: Eliminate parent investment vs sub equity
   - Unrealized profit: Defer IC inventory markup until external sale
   - Basis normalization: Adjust for group accounting standards
   - Translation: Record CTA to OCI
   - Minority interest: Allocate NCI share of net assets/income
   
   Key: Live on consolidation layer (not in entity books)
        Entity books remain source of truth
        Full audit trail of what was adjusted

5. STATE MACHINE

   ConsolidationRun.status:
   
   IN_PROGRESS ─→ COMPLETE (if no blocking issues)
                 ↓
              BLOCKED (if open periods, unresolved IC mismatches)
                 ↓
              FINALIZED (after review/approval)

6. AUDIT TRAIL

   All consolidation adjustments logged:
   - Created by (user)
   - Created at (timestamp)
   - Adjustment type (elimination, translation, etc.)
   - Description (what & why)
   - Immutable: Cannot edit after applied
   - Reversals: New entries that offset originals

================================================================================
BLOCKING CONDITIONS
================================================================================

Consolidation CANNOT proceed if:
  1. Any entity in scope has OPEN periods before consolidation date
  2. Any intercompany transaction is MISMATCHED and not manually RESOLVED
  3. Parent entity not found or no ownership link to entities in scope
  4. (Warning only) Entity accounts missing consolidation mappings

Mismatches are resolved via:
  - Correct the source entry (post reversal + correction)
  - Approve variance (override with explanation)
  - Exclude from consolidation (document reason)
